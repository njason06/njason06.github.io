How to use your pieces cohesively

The first step is to develop your pieces. You need to move your pieces from the bottom rank (the bottom rank is where your king is). This is called development, if your pieces are in the backrow they are unable to do anything. You can learn basic openings to help with development. I reccomend learning the London system or the Italian game. These are really beginner friendly and help you use your pieces cohesively.

How to Attack

Attacking in Chess is pretty difficult. Somehow making your pieces work together is actually pretty hard. An easy way to get your pieces to work together is creating a common goal. This could either be trying to win a piece, or attacking the king. Having your pieces work together is crucial because if there isn't another piece protecting each other, the king will either be able to take or another piece will be able to take with no consequences.
